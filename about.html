#CSBYALL

#CSBYALL is a group of student led initiative who worked with their client mentor CSforAll throughout a 6 week course in summer.#CSBYALL is a partner project of CSforAll. The goal of #CSBYALL is to showcase some projects of some young computer scientists on some diverse fields and inspire other students to try another flavour, Computer Science.


CSforAll

Computer Science for All(csforall) is a bold new initiative to empower all US students from kindergarten through high school to learn computer science and be equipped with the computational thinking skills they need to be creators in the digital economy, not just consumers, and to be active citizens in our technology-driven world. Our economy is rapidly shifting, and educators, policy makers, families, and business leaders are increasingly recognizing that computer science (CS) is a “new basic” skill necessary for economic opportunity and social mobility.


Our Goal

#CSBYALL and CSforAll both shares a common goal, which is to provide with the resources to learn Computer science to k-12 schools in USA. Computer Science is connected to everything. From creating the tools for an alarm clock to wake you up to going to bed with turning the lights off, everything requires a computer scientist.


Be a part of the one of the fastest growing industry field, help yourself and your school to get Computer Science,a major interest in the industry.
